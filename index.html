About Startup Domain Search

Startup Domain Search is a free tool for founders and indie hackers that goes beyond just generating and checking names — it helps you score and choose the best one. Describe your startup idea, get 60 AI-generated domain candidates, and see each one objectively ranked so you can pick with confidence.

How scoring works: Star ★ any domain to add it to the Favorites panel. Every favorited name is scored on six dimensions — LEN (shorter = better), PRO (easy to say aloud), MEM (memorable, avoids generic patterns), BRD (brandable, invented/unique), ZON (available TLDs), and FIT (AI-scored relevance to your idea). Adjust the weights to match what matters most to you, and the TOTAL column ranks your shortlist.

How to use: Type your startup idea, select TLD zones (.ai, .com, .io, .app, .dev, etc.), click "Search Domains" and results stream in. Star names you like, tune the score weights, enter a FIT context for AI relevance scoring, then save your shortlist.

API key: Uses a bundled Groq key by default — no setup needed. Paste your own Groq, OpenAI, or Anthropic key in the "AI provider" field for heavy use. Keys are stored only in your browser.

Privacy: All data lives in your browser's localStorage. Nothing goes to any server except direct AI API calls and RDAP availability checks to rdap.org. No account, no tracking, no data collected.
